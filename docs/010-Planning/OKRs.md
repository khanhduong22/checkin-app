# Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) - 2026

## Objective 1: Operational Excellence
**Goal**: Reduce manual administrative overhead by 50%.
- **KR 1**: Achieve 95% accuracy in automated payroll calculation (vs manual adjustment).
- **KR 2**: Reduce average time to approve leave requests from 24h to 4h via mobile notifications.
- **KR 3**: Eliminate 100% of "buddy punching" via IP/Device validation.

## Objective 2: Employee Engagement
**Goal**: Make work fun and rewarding to increase retention.
- **KR 1**: Achieve 80% daily active usage (DAU) of the Lucky Wheel feature.
- **KR 2**: Maintain average Shop Pet health > 80% across all users.
- **KR 3**: Increase Task Marketplace completion rate by 30% MoM.

## Objective 3: System Reliability
**Goal**: Ensure the system is trusted and always available.
- **KR 1**: Maintain 99.9% uptime for Check-in services between 8 AM - 10 AM.
- **KR 2**: Zero critical data loss incidents for attendance records.
