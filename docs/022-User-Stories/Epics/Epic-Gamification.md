# Epic: Gamification

## Summary
Increases employee engagement through fun, interactive elements that reward consistent attendance and task completion.

## Features / User Stories

### 1. Lucky Wheel
- **As a User**, I want to spin a wheel once a day to win prizes.
- **Constraint**: Must have checked in today to spin.
- **Constraint**: Prizes are limited by quantity and probability.

### 2. Shop Pet (Tamagotchi)
- **As a User**, I want to see a virtual pet that reacts to my activity.
- **Logic**: Pet gains XP/Health when I check in on time or complete tasks.
- **Logic**: Pet loses mood/health if I am absent or late.

### 3. Achievements
- **As a User**, I want to earn badges for milestones (e.g., "Early Bird", "Task Master").
- **As a User**, I want to see my achievements on my profile.
